Biography

Buxx is an emerging DJ with bags of talent and a keen ear to provide appropriate sets for any venue. With a clear passion for music, seamless mixing skills, thoughtful track selection and an understanding for what makes the crowd tick Buxx will be able to transform your dancefloor and keep people coming back for more. Whether its hard-hitting techno you are looking for, chilled out melodic vibes, feel good afrohouse, commercial vocal house or Deep House crowd pleasers Buxx is your guy.

After attending some of the world’s biggest parties and having a genuine connection with all forms of House music Buxx had gone on to complete a professional DJ course with Granular DXB. Buxx has also further pursued his passion for music gaining knowledge whilst dabbling with the world of music production completing a course with the world recognised Point Blank Music School.

Buxx takes inspiration from a wide variety of Artists all with their own flavours and suitability for different types of events. Examples include Black Coffee, Hot Since 82, Eelke Kleijn, Dusky, Yotto, Adriatique and The Martinez Brothers to name just a few. Paying close attention to the current trends of Electronic Music and recent hits means sets will be ever changing and relevant on any given week whilst not ignoring timeless Music from the past.
